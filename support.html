Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate a tip?

Enter your bill amount in the Calculator tab and tap one of the tip percentage cards (15%, 20%, 25%) to see the tip and total amount. Tap any card to copy the total to your clipboard.

How do I split a bill?

Go to Settings → Split Tip and set the number of people. The app will automatically calculate per-person amounts and display them on the tip cards.

How do I change the currency?

Go to Settings → Currency and select your preferred currency from the list. All amounts will be displayed in your chosen currency.

How do I use Smart Tip?

Go to the Smart Tip tab, enter your bill amount, select the service type (Restaurant, Bar, Delivery, etc.), set the service quality, and the app will provide a personalized tip recommendation based on current tipping standards.

How do I scan a receipt?

Tap the "Scan" button next to the bill input field and point your camera at the receipt. The app will automatically extract the total amount.

How do I customize tip percentages?

Go to Settings → Tip Presets and tap on any preset to edit the percentage and label.

How do I share tip calculations?

Tap the share icon on any tip card, or use the "Send to a friend" button when splitting bills. You can share via Messages, Email, or other apps.

Does the app work offline?

Yes! iTip works completely offline. No internet connection is required for any features.

Is my data private?

Yes! All data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Your privacy is our priority.

Is the app free?

Yes! iTip Smart Tip Calculator is completely free to use with no subscriptions or hidden fees.
